## Study title

Visually-3D: Spatial Causal Visualization for Diagnosing LLM Agent Failures

## What you will do

You will use 3–5 different visualization tools to inspect logs from LLM agent
runs that failed on coding or web-automation benchmarks. For each run you will
identify the most likely root cause, the first failure point, and a repair
strategy, and answer brief subjective questions.

Estimated session length: **60–90 minutes**.

## Data we collect

- Your responses (root cause, first failure point, repair type, confidence).
- Time spent per task.
- Mouse/keyboard interaction log inside the study UI.
- Subjective questionnaires (NASA-TLX, SUS, trust).

We do **not** record screen content outside the study UI, audio, or video.

## Storage

Data is stored under an opaque participant ID. The mapping from ID to identity
is kept separately and destroyed on study completion.

## Your rights

- You may request deletion of your data up to publication submission.
- You may ask any question before, during, or after the session.
